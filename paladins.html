Hello everyone, and welcome to my guide on the game paladins, by Hi-Rez studios. Please note that I do not own the game and am only explaining about the game for educational purposes. Without further distraction, lets get into the guide.

First of all what kind of game is Paladins? Paladins is a first person shooter video game developed by Evil Mojo Games (development studio under the Hi-Rez Studios publishing group). It consists of different playable characters (champions) that each have unique abilities they bring to the field. Each character has a role that they specialize in which revolves around how the character is meant to be played.

The Different Roles

There are 4 roles of characters in Paladins which consist of frontlines, damages, supports and flanks. They each have their pros and cons that can easily be distinguished.

Frontline Damage Healer Flank
-the ideal objective holder -teammate protector -gain more credits for standing near an objective -can be mowed down by damage if lacks healing -champions that can consistently deal high amounts of damage -gain for credits for damaging enemy champions -can be killed by flanks if not watching their six -champions that can play offensively and defensively -gain more credits for healing allies -the ideal target for flanks to kill (usually breaks a team) -champions that excel in both damage and mobility to help them in combat as well as chase and confirm kills from the enemy's rear -gain more credits from landing killing blows on enemies -low health and can be killed easily
